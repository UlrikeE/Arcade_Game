## Instructions

This is a classic arcade game. The player has to match two cards. If two cards match, they remain open. When all cards have been matched the player wins the game. The timer and the number of moves, also shown by the star rating, indicate the player's performance.

In order to win the game, you have to earn 100 points.
To earn points you have to help me get to the other side of the field and pick flowers.
You have 3 lifes and 1 bonus life. If you collide with a tractor you loose a life.
If you get run over by a tractor 4 times, you cannot help me - you loose.

## Install

Open in any browser.
